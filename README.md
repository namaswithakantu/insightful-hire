# Insightful Hiring

## Overview

**Insightful Hiring** is an AI-powered interview assessment platform designed to provide candidates with a realistic interview experience while ensuring interview integrity through live proctoring. The platform allows users to select an interview topic, answer questions through voice or text, and receive detailed performance analysis after the interview.

The system monitors candidate behavior during the interview by detecting activities such as tab switching and copy-paste attempts while using the camera and microphone for proctoring. At the end of the interview, it evaluates responses, assigns scores, provides constructive feedback, and displays progress through an interactive dashboard.

---

## Features

### Topic-Based Interview

* Select an interview topic based on your area of interest.
* AI-generated interview questions related to the chosen topic.

### Interactive Interview Experience

* Answer questions using:

  * Voice input
  * Text input
* Smooth and user-friendly interview interface.

### AI Proctoring

To maintain interview integrity, the platform monitors:

* Camera activity during the interview.
* Microphone usage.
* Copy (Ctrl + C) and Paste (Ctrl + V) attempts.
* Tab switching and window focus changes.
* Interview policy violations.

### Response Evaluation

After completing the interview, the platform:

* Evaluates each response.
* Assigns question-wise scores.
* Calculates the overall performance score.
* Identifies strengths and areas for improvement.
* Provides personalized feedback.

### Performance Dashboard

The dashboard enables users to:

* View interview history.
* Track performance over time.
* Compare previous interview scores.
* Analyze question-wise performance.
* Monitor overall improvement.

---

## Workflow

1. User logs into the platform.
2. Selects an interview topic.
3. Grants camera and microphone permissions.
4. Interview begins.
5. The system monitors interview violations in real time.
6. User answers questions using voice or text.
7. AI evaluates the responses.
8. A detailed report is generated.
9. Results and analytics are displayed on the dashboard.
